Omni Cancun Hotel and Villas

Key Largo, Florida

All-Inclusives Right Here in The US

  • by Admin
  • 3 min read

Bungalows Key Largo in Key Largo Florida is an adults-only haven in the Florida Keys. Spend your stay in one the resorts beautiful waterfront or garden bungalows. All-inclusive pricing covers all meals and top-shelf beverage options at specified restaraunts and bars. Also included are daily group yoga classes outdoors on their Tranquility Lawn or Coconut Beach while listening to ocean waves. Or take advantage of the use of kayaks, paddleboards, snorkel gear and water trikes which are included with your package.

Paradise Island, Bahamas

Atlantis Resort

  • by Admin
  • 3 min read

Although the Atlantis Resort doesn't include meals, this resort's vacation package does include full access to all Atlantis water park features at no additional cost. This includedes 11 swimming areas, 11 water slides, two Lazy River Rides, Paradise Lagoon, The Dig, the Predator's Lagoon, all of their marine life habitats, and the ocean beach area. Beach towels are also provided daily at no charge as long as you are inside the water park. Want to include meals? Just purchase one of their meal plans to complete your package.

When you go to places like Disney, you have to pay for your hotel and still pay extra for access to parks. It's nice that the kids could enjoy all the water activities at Atlantis everyday without an additional charge.

A. Smith
