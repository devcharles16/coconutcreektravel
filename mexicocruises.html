Mexico Cruise Ports

  • by Admin
  • 3 min read

Mexico is filled with Mayan Ruins and native cultural experiences. You can experience Mexico's culture in cities such as Cozumel, Costa Maya,and Cancun. Here are some of the top excursions and activities in the most popular cruise ports in Mexico.

  • 1. Cozumel
    Cozumel is great for beach days, culinary experiences, and adventurous activities like ATV riding, zipling and parasailing.
    Here are some of our top excursions in Mexico:
    • Beach Day - Cozumel's beach day excursions offer beach days that include access to beaches and water parks. The cost of these excursions usually include beach chairs and umbrellas, snorkel equipment and meals and drinks.
    • 4 Wheelers and ATV's -
      • Take a tour in an open door Jeep Wrangler or Dune Buggy off road.
    • Mahogany Bay, Honduras
    • Nassau and Freeport, Bahamas
    • Grand Turk, Turks and Caicos
    • Ocho Rios, Jamaica
    • Belize, Central America
    • San Juan, Puerto Rico
    • St. Kitts, West Indies
    • St. Thomas, US Virgin Islands
    • Aruba, Bonaire and Curacao, Netherland Antilles
    • La Romana, Dominican Republic
    • Various Private Islands and Cays throughout the Caribbean
  • 2. Norwegian Cruise Lines
    Norwegian Cruise Line sails the Caribbean from Miami, New Orleans, New York, Orlando, Panama City, San Juan and Tampa.
    Ports included on these sailings are:
    • Cozumel and Costa Maya Mexico
    • Tortola, British Virgin Islands
    • George Town, Grand Cayman
    • Nassau, Bahamas
    • Grand Turk, Turks and Caicos
    • Ocho Rios, Jamaica
    • Roatan, Bay Islands
    • San Juan, Puerto Rico
    • St. Kitts, West Indies
    • St. Thomas, US Virgin Islands
    • Aruba, Bonaire and Curacao, Netherland Antilles
    • Puerto Plata, Dominican Republic
    • St. Lucia, West Indies
    • Grenada, West Indies
    • Barbados, West Indies
    • Antigua, West Indies
    • Key West, Florida
    • Puerto Limon, Costa Rica
    • Colon, Panama
    • Various Private Islands and Cays throughout the Caribbean
  • 2. Royal Caribbean
    Royal Caribbean sails the Caribbean from Miami, Baltimore, Orlando, Galveston, Cape Liberty and Tampa.
    Ports included on these sailings are:
    • Cozumel and Costa Maya, Mexico
    • Roatan, Bay Islands, Honduras
    • Labadee, Haiti
    • George Town, Grand Cayman
    • Nassau and Grand Bahama Island, Bahamas
    • Falmouth, Jamaica
    • San Juan, Puerto Rico
    • St. Kitts, West Indies
    • St. Thomas and St. Croix, US Virgin Islands
    • Aruba, Bonaire and Curacao, Netherland Antilles
    • Puerto Plata, Dominican Republic
    • St. Lucia, West Indies
    • Grenada, West Indies
    • Barbados, West Indies
    • Antigua, West Indies
    • Puerto Limon, Costa Rica
    • Colon, Panama
    • Cartagena, Columbia
    • Belize City, Belize
    • Various Private Islands and Cays throughout the Caribbean

No matter which cruise line you choose, you are sure to have a great time. A Caribbean cruise is a great way to enjoy multiple Caribbean islands in one trip.
